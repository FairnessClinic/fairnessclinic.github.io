About Fairness Clinic

Fairness Clinic is a not for profit community service. We provide free, confidential conversations for workers who feel they’ve been treated unfairly at work. We operate a mobile office across the GTA and meet people at community centres, libraries, and partner sites.

Projects: Fairness at Work is a flagship project of the Clinic focused on workplace rights and systemic advocacy. Other projects will be added as we grow.

When we escalate

If a matter requires licensed representation, we will explain options and, with your consent, refer you to a qualified professional. Paid litigation is handled through our litigation channel at fairnessclinic.com. Our free service remains independent and confidential.

Who we help

  • Workers experiencing unfair treatment, discrimination, or taken advantage of
  • Employers seeking to build fair workplaces and avoid expensive disputes
  • Community partners and service providers looking to maintain healthy and fair local communities

Ethics and privacy

Conversations are confidential. We do not share your information without consent except where required by law. Fairness Clinic is committed to transparent referrals and clear consent before any paid legal engagement.
